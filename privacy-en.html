Privacy Policy

Magical Stories for Kids: AI

Last Updated: February 2025

Welcome to Magical Stories for Kids: AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our services:

  • Story Preferences: Age range, language selection, story categories, and story length preferences to generate appropriate content.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about app usage to improve our service.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe, payment is processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not store payment details.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for children ages 2-12, used with parental supervision. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

  • We do not collect personal information from children.
  • We do not require registration or login for basic app usage.
  • We do not display third-party advertisements.
  • Stories are generated locally and are not linked to any personal identity.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Generate age-appropriate stories based on selected preferences.
  • Provide AI voice narration for stories.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.
  • Process subscriptions through Apple's secure payment system.

Data Storage and Security

Story preferences are stored locally on your device. We use industry-standard security measures to protect any data transmitted to our servers for story generation.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • AI Story Generation: We use AI services to create stories. No personal data is shared with these services.
  • Apple App Store: For processing subscriptions and payments.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete locally stored story history at any time within the app.
  • Cancel your subscription through your Apple ID settings.
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app or our website.
