Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone, then select Magical Stories to cancel.

Can I use the app offline?

Story generation requires an internet connection, but previously generated stories can be accessed offline.

What ages is this app for?

Our app is designed for children ages 2-12, with parental supervision recommended.

How do I restore my purchase?

If the app cannot find your subscription, a "Restore Purchase" button will appear automatically. Tap it to restore your subscription.
